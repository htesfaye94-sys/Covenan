The Sacred Mission

Our mission is the intellectual preservation and defense of the Oriental Orthodox faith. We strive to bridge the divide between ancient Patristic wisdom and modern philosophical discourse, providing a sanctuary for rigorous inquiry and spiritual devotion.

About

Dedicated to the study of the Church Fathers, specifically the Christology of St. Cyril of Alexandria and St. Severus of Antioch. As a student of Meta-physics and Philosophy, the work here explores the necessary existence of the Creator and the profound depths of the Incarnate Word.
